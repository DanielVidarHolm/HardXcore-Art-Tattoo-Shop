Our Story

In November 2021, Poul Albert established HardXcore Tattoo shop , in New York. The goal was to build a space that brought artists together to collaborate in a way that would positively influence the community, right in his hometown of New Cape town. Our mission is to blend parts of the past and the future, historical connections with a unique, modern twist. Our vision is to advance the art form. We are committed to creating one-of-a-kind tattoos designed specifically for each individual, alongside an unparalleled client experience. At its inception, the shop featured just three artists, but as the demand for HardXcore Tattoo shop designs grew, the need for more artists and a second location became inevitable. HardXcore Tattoo shop NYC opened its doors in April 2011 on the street that played a formative role in tattoo history, The Bowery, which overlooks some of Manhattan’s most prominent cultural centers.

As the two busy shops drew increasing respect and critical acclaim throughout the industry, HardXcore Tattoo shop’s family of artists expanded. More than a dozen resident artists, in addition to a rotation of sought-after guests – including Tim Hendricks, Horitomo, and Henning Jorgensen – have worked with clients across the globe, catapulting HardXcore Tattoo shop into the top-tier of tattoo studios. In April 2022, HardXcore Tattoo shop expanded once more with the opening of its third location in the Downtown Historic district of Durham, North Carolina.But even as our family continues to grow in both size and reputation, HardXcore Tattoo shop remains focused on integrity and quality, The professional artists at HardXcore Tattoo shop are hand-selected from all over the world and specialize in many styles, ranging from Japanese, American traditional, black and gray, to realism. They thrive on creating unique and original works of art.Our tattooers have been shaped into who they are partly due to an unwavering commitment to their core values.

Why I became a tattoo artist
